Who we are

PFC Mortgages was founded in 2000. We moved into our Beazer House office in Bath and launched our first website in 2001. Winning our first award for best mortgage broker in 2002, we’ve gone from strength to strength, with further offices in Newcastle Upon Tyne and London, and over 2 million people coming to us for advice on the best mortgage for them. Find out more about our leadership and colleagues by taking a look at Our People.

We’re proud to support our company charity, Winston’s Wish, the UK's leading childhood bereavement charity. Every year Winston’s Wish need to raise over £2.4 million to provide practical help and support to children suffering the loss of a parent or sibling. For every life policy we arrange we donate £1 and we’ve now raised over £50,000 to Winston’s Wish. For more information visit the Winston's Wish website.

What we do

All the hard work for you! Our aim is to make applying for a mortgage a simple and hassle-free experience for our customers – whether you’re buying your first home, moving house, remortgaging or investing in a Buy to Let property.

01

Get started by completing our online Mortgage Finder or speaking to us over the phone.

02

Your adviser will search across the market to find the best deal for your circumstances.

03

We’ll help finish off the paperwork and get your application started.

04

Track your case with your online Portal 24/7 and easily message your case manager.

We’re here for you from application all the way through to completion, and the best bit is we won't charge you a fee for our advice and support. We'll search across the market with access to 1000s of deals from over 90 lenders, so we'll be sure to find the right deal for you.
Wondering how our service is fee free? Like all other brokers, we receive a payment from the lender when the mortgage completes. The difference is that we simply choose not to charge our customers a fee on top of this.
